# The Wound — Solari Side Artwork Prompt

## Core Concept

A wide cinematic scene showing **the Wound** from the **Solari side**, as if the viewer is standing at eye level behind a small group of Solari soldiers or adventurers approaching the shore.

The Wound was created by a magical weapon that pushed matter, life, magic, bodies, and souls partially or completely beyond existence. The area is not merely ruined or dead; it is a place where reality has been erased, thinned, and left unstable. Those affected are not truly dead, but not alive either.

## Final Artwork Direction

**Format:** wide landscape composition, **800 px × 320 px**  
**Camera:** eye-level view, viewer positioned behind the group  
**Subject:** three Solari soldiers/adventurers seen from behind  
**Mood:** gloomy daylight, ominous, supernatural, desaturated but crisp  
**Style:** high-end cinematic fantasy illustration, semi-realistic, modern D&D sourcebook cover art  

## Main Prompt

Create a wide 800px by 320px cinematic fantasy landscape illustration in a semi-realistic modern D&D sourcebook cover art style. The camera is at eye level, positioned behind three Solari soldiers or adventurers standing on the eastern bank of the Wound, looking out across the eerie waters. Keep the figures in the foreground, seen from behind, wearing weathered armor and dark travel cloaks, with crisp detail on helmets, armor plates, fabric folds, belts, weapons, and silhouettes.

The scene is daylight but gloomy: heavy overcast sky, dim cold light, muted colors, misty atmosphere, and a sense of dread. The Wound stretches before them like a wide flooded rift or unnatural gulf, calm but wrong, with faint ripples, ghostly reflections, and subtle distortions where matter seems partially erased. Dead Trees Forest remnants appear on both sides of the Wound: twisted black dead trunks, drowned roots, half-submerged trees, broken branches, and scattered lifeless copses along the shore.

Use dramatic lighting with cool grey-blue ambient daylight and a faint warm supernatural glow rising from the water, creating restrained rim light on the soldiers and the dead trees. Add subtle magical effects over the water: drifting golden embers, faint sigil-like wisps, thin luminous cracks, and small areas where the shoreline or tree trunks appear cleanly absent instead of broken, as if reality was cut away. The background should show misty hills and distant dark mountains beyond the water.

The overall image should feel ominous, ancient, and awe-inspiring, not bright or heroic. Keep the details crisp and readable, especially on the soldiers, trees, shoreline, and magical glow, while the far background remains softer and atmospheric.

## Negative Prompt / Avoid

Avoid night scene, bright cheerful daylight, saturated sunny fantasy colors, cartoon style, anime style, blurry details, soft focus foreground, extreme darkness, excessive fog hiding the composition, extra characters, close-up portrait framing, top-down view, low-angle heroic pose, modern clothing, buildings, clean living forest, lush greenery, boats, bridges, text, symbols as UI, watermarks, logos.
