The Council of the Crown of Verosia is a group of highly skilled and powerful individuals who serve as advisors to the king. Each member specializes in a particular area of expertise, from magic and tactics to naturalism and diplomacy. 

The council is led by [[High Magus Zephyrion]] and includes [[Archmage Arcturus]], [[Lady Celestria, Voice of the Heavens]], [[Lord Valerian - Master of Tactics]], [[Archdruidess Ylara,]] [[Grand Inquisitor Darius, Keeper of the Faith]], [[Lady Isadora - Mistress of the Arcane Arts]], [[Sir Galen, Knight of the Golden Sun]], [[Master Artificer Thorne, Keeper of the Clockwork Secrets]], [[Lady Althea, Mistress of Poisons and Cures]], and [[Archsage Alder]].

Together, they help guide the kingdom and ensure its prosperity and security. The council meets regularly to discuss matters of importance, from political and military strategy to matters of faith and justice. Their expertise and knowledge are highly valued by the king, and they are considered among the most influential and respected individuals in the realm. However, their positions also make them targets for those who seek to undermine the kingdom or seize power for themselves. As such, the council is constantly vigilant and prepared to defend the kingdom against any threats that may arise.
