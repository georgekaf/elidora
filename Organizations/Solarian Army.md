The Solarian Army is a disciplined and formidable military force, renowned for its unwavering loyalty to the Empire of Solari and its mastery of advanced warfare tactics. Comprised of heavily armored infantry, skilled archers, and elite cavalry, the army is further bolstered by battlemages who wield destructive magic on the battlefield. Their golden sun insignia reflects their devotion to the Empire’s ideology of light and order. Known for their relentless precision and unyielding resolve, the Solarian Army is a force to be reckoned with, feared by all who dare to oppose the Empire.
