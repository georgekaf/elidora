## **Former Guardian (Interventionist Survivor)**

### **Name (Suggested):**

- **Vaelith Duskbane** – evokes secrecy, shadowed knowledge, and moral ambiguity.

### **Background:**

- Vaelith was originally a Guardian aligned with the **Interventionist faction** before **The Sundering of the Guardians** an part of the innercircle that directly performed the rituals and creation of the sphere.
- During the war between Verosia and Solari, Vaelith witnessed firsthand the devastation caused by mortals and the misuse of the Nexus Souls.
- After the schism, Vaelith went into hiding, keeping fragments of Guardian knowledge and the secrets of the Nexus Tracker.

### **Connection to Arcturus:**

- Vaelith encountered Arcturus centuries later, seeing in him a mortal with ambition, intelligence, and the willingness to act where the Guardians would not.
- Vaelith became Arcturus’ **mentor, strategist, and guide**, teaching him:
    - The location and manipulation of Nexus Souls
    - The rituals for bloodline memory transfer
    - Methods to evade the Shrouded Path and surviving Preservationists
### **Personality and Motivations:**

- **Calculating and secretive:** Vaelith trusts almost no one, even Arcturus fully.
- **Ideologically rigid:** Believes that Nexus Souls must be used proactively to stabilize the multiverse, even if it risks mortal lives.
- **Cynical about mortals:** Sees kingdoms, rulers, and councils as weak, short-sighted, or corruptible.
- **Long-term goal:** Achieve a multiverse “stability” on their own terms, with or without Arcturus.

### **Abilities and Influence:**

- **Arcane Mastery:** Immense magical power, enhanced by centuries of experience and access to ancient Guardian knowledge.
- **Manipulator:** Skilled in politics, espionage, and subtle influence across kingdoms.
- **Nexus Soul Expertise:** Can locate, manipulate, or extract Nexus Souls with knowledge unavailable to ordinary mortals.

### **Campaign Role:**

1. **Hidden Antagonist:** Operates in the shadows, manipulating Arcturus and mortal factions.
2. **Moral Foil:** Represents the extreme Interventionist ideology, showing what happens if the Nexus Souls are used recklessly.
3. **Potential Reveal:** Heroes may uncover Vaelith’s identity mid-campaign, revealing a centuries-long manipulation.
4. **Direct Threat:** Could intervene personally if the heroes interfere too much with Arcturus’ plans, becoming a powerful boss encounter.
