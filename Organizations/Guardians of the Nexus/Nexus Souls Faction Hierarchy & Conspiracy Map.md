

### **1. Shrouded Path (Preservationists)**

- **Location:** Athum Spires, deep inside the Sultanate of Zephyr.
- **Philosophy:** Protect Nexus Souls from misuse; intervene minimally.
- **Key Goals:**
    - Preserve fragments of Guardian knowledge.
    - Maintain bloodline memory rituals.
    - Prevent Interventionists and mortals from destabilizing the multiverse.
- **Structure:**
    - **High Custodian:** Leader of the Shrouded Path; oldest surviving member.
    - **Circle of Shadows:** Senior members overseeing regions, artifacts, and bloodline heirs.
    - **Keepers and Initiates:** Field agents, scholars, and mystics who safeguard secrets.
- **Methods:** Secrecy, manipulation, subterfuge, cryptic guidance to mortals.

---

### **2. Interventionists (Rogue Faction)**

- **Philosophy:** Actively use Nexus Souls to stabilize or reshape the multiverse, regardless of mortal cost.
- **Fate After the Sundering:**
    
    - Most went into hiding after failed attempts during the Verosia-Solari war.
    - Operate as secret cults, planar exiles, or hidden agents within kingdoms.
- **Key Figures:**
    
    - **Vaelith Duskbane** – Former Guardian, Interventionist Survivor, mentor to Arcturus.
    - **Rogue Cells:** Smaller splinter groups interpreting Interventionist philosophy differently, some seeking power, others knowledge.
- **Methods:** Direct action, manipulation, ritual magic, alliances with ambitious mortals.

---

### **3. Archmage Arcturus**

- **Role:** Mortal agent influenced and mentored by Vaelith.
- **Goal:** Recover Nexus Souls and manipulate events, ostensibly for Solari or personal ambition.
- **Methods:**
    - Kidnaps heirs like Arielle to access bloodline memories.
    - Uses Solari-aligned agents to retrieve Nexus Souls.
    - Attempts to evade both the Shrouded Path and Interventionist rivals.

---

### **4. Mortal Agents**

- **Solari-Aligned Spies & Scholars:** Act as Arcturus’ operatives across Elidora.
    
- **Bloodline Heirs:** Potential carriers of Nexus Tracker memories; targets for Arcturus or Interventionists.
    
- **Hidden Allies/Traitors:** Mortals who may secretly assist Preservationists or Interventionists depending on ideology.
    

---

### **5. Cosmic Layer**

- **Nexus Souls:** Core objects of power, each carrying fragments of reality’s stability.
    
- **Planar/Extradimensional Threats:** Interventionists or rogue entities may tap into these realms for additional power.
    
- **Moral Dilemma:** Heroes’ choices influence whether Nexus Souls are protected, used, or abused.
