
- **Domain:** War and Domination
- **Description:** A brutal god of conquest and subjugation, Kalthorak sought to rule all of existence. He was overthrown by a coalition of gods and mortals who united against his tyranny. His death shattered his iron throne and freed countless enslaved spirits.
- **Legacy:** His weapons of war still linger, cursed and scattered across Elidora, sowing discord among those who wield them.
