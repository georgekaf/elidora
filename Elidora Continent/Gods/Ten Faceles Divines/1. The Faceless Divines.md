This pantheon consists of gods that are worshiped without strict domains, embodying broad concepts and ideals rather than specific aspects of existence. They are venerated across Elidora by various cultures, each interpreting their purposes in unique ways.

### **Roleplaying Note**

These gods are not tied to specific alignments or rigid dogmas, allowing for flexible interpretation. Worshippers often venerate multiple deities depending on their needs or philosophies. The gods themselves are seen as enigmatic forces, less involved in mortal affairs than traditional pantheons.


All other Gods (Toril pantheon, Egyptian, Norse, Greek) are known as Unknown Deities, that have revealed themeselves to individuals in an attempt to gain more followers and power.
