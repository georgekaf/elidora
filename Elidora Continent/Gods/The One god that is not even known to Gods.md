**One God, The Veilpiercer**

- **Domains:** Knowledge, Arcana, Twilight
- **Appearance:** Nytherion is often depicted as a tall, androgynous figure cloaked in a shifting robe of stars and nebulae. Their face is obscured by an ever-changing cosmic veil, revealing only glimpses of infinite galaxies beneath. They carry an intricate staff tipped with a crystalline eye that peers into the essence of all things.  
- **Symbol:** A stylized eye set within a spiral of stars, representing the pursuit of truth hidden in the cosmos.
- **Tenets of Worship:**
    - Seek the hidden truths of the universe, no matter how unsettling they may be.
    - Embrace both the light and shadow of knowledge; truth often lies in the in-between.
    - Preserve the balance of the cosmic order by unraveling and respecting its mysteries.
- **Legends:** Nytherion is said to have torn through the fabric of reality to peer into the First Truth, an act that granted them ultimate knowledge but cost them their own identity. Their followers believe that Nytherion now exists beyond the comprehension of mortals, guiding those who seek to uncover the deeper truths of existence.
- **Worshipers:** Seekers of knowledge, astronomers, philosophers, and arcane scholars. They often refer to Nytherion as "The Eye Beyond the Stars" or "The Keeper of the Eternal Mystery."
