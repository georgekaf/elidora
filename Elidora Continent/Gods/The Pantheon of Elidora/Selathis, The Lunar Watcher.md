
- **Domain:** Knowledge, Mysticism, Secrets
- **Alignment:** Neutral
- **Symbol:** A crescent moon with a single star above it
- **Description:** Selathis is the deity of the moon, mysteries, and hidden truths. Often worshiped by scholars, spies, and explorers, Selathis encourages curiosity and the pursuit of wisdom while respecting the balance between what is revealed and what is hidden.
