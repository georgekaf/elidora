# The Federation of Orlais — Snapshot

A coastal archipelago-and-peninsula federation of city-states and small duchies that banded together for mutual defense and trade. Polished courts, patronage of the arts, and razor-edged diplomacy define Orlais. It borders **Valoria** to the west and sits astride lucrative sea lanes.

## Government & Power

- **Type:** Federal league of principalities/cities under the **Conclave of Orlais** (bicameral):
    
    - **Council of Banners** (rulers or envoys of member states; votes by state).
        
    - **Council of Guilds** (merchants, shipwrights, bankers, artisans; votes by weight of charter).
        
- **First Arbiter:** Rotating presiding magistrate (1-year term) who breaks ties and commands the **Federal Guard** in crises.
    
- **Law:** Members retain internal law; federal competencies: **treaties, customs union, navy, roads/ports standards, coinage**, and mutual defense.
    
- **Checks:** Any war beyond defense needs **dual-council majority**; sanctions for defectors are tariff hikes and loss of convoy protection.
    

## Economy (what pays for the lace and the marble)

|Sector|What Orlais Does Best|Notes|
|---|---|---|
|**Maritime Trade & Finance**|Convoys, insurance, banking houses, letters of exchange|Conclave regulates bills of credit; trusted clearinghouses|
|**Shipbuilding**|Fast caravels, sleek galleasses, river barges|Guild standards; famous Orlaisan pitch & sailcloth|
|**Textiles & Fashion**|Fine linen, brocade, dyes, lace|Court demand across Elidora; seasonal fairs|
|**Glass & Crafts**|Stained glass, optics, jewelry, musical instruments|Exports to Verosia/Valoria; status goods|
|**Wine & Olive Oils**|Terraced vineyards on warm isles|Premium vintages fund politics|
|**Printing & Drama**|Illustrated chapbooks, court plays|Soft-power export; sometimes “banned in Solari” (bonus sales)|

**Money/Tax:** Silver **écu**, gold **or**, copper **sou**. Low external tariffs, internal **customs union**; harbor dues fund lighthouses and federal convoys. Merchant banks issue **Conclave bills**—nearly as good as coin.

## Military & Security

- **Federal Navy:** Blue-and-white pennants; fast escort squadrons for convoys.
    
- **City Militias & Contract Knights:** Members keep forces but must muster quotas on Conclave call.
    
- **Doctrine:** Protect trade first; fight to disengage, not to annihilate. Privateers licensed in wartime (strict prize courts).
    

## Diplomacy (default stances)

- **Valoria:** Cordial neighbors; Orlais buys sanctified metals, sells luxury goods.
    
- **Verosia:** Friendly—scholar exchanges, finance, and ship contracts.
    
- **Solari:** Polite frost. Trade is welcome; censorship, quotas, and “advisers” are not.
    
- **Drakonia:** Respect pilots; buy pearls/resins; hire escorts when crossing Medeia.
    

## Culture

- **Aesthetic:** Sky-blue banners, white doves and clasped hands; public fountains, theater courts, masked salons.
    
- **Values:** Civility, contract, elegance, and the art of the well-timed favor.
    
- **Faith:** Plural—patron saints of craft and sea; dove as civic emblem of concord.
    

## Names (Orlais style)

- **Masculine:** Étienne, Lucien, Marceau, Armand, Bastien, Roland, Cyrille, Valère
    
- **Feminine:** Élodie, Sabine, Mireille, Colette, Giselle, Aurélie, Maëlle, Sylvie
    
- **Surnames:** de Valmont, Deschamps, Beauregard, Lemaire, D’Aubigny, Morel, Fontaine, Chevalier
    
- **Courtesy styles:** _Seigneur/Seigneuresse_, _Marquis/Marquise_, _Doyen/Doyenne_ (guild heads).
    

## Adventure Hooks (quick)

1. **Convoy in Peril:** A Conclave-insured gold shipment must cross a Solari patrol zone; PCs escort or outfox privateers.
    
2. **Masks & Daggers:** A masked salon hides a vote-buying ring; expose it without sparking a federation split.
    
3. **Banned Play:** A satirical drama about Solari vanishes on opening night; retrieve script and actors before the censors.
    
4. **Banker’s Bill:** A forged Conclave bill threatens Orlais credit; trace the plates to an Arathori mint-room.
