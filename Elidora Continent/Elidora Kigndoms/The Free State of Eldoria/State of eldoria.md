# Overview

- **Type:** Loose **federation of communes** and refuge-towns, born from uprisings and desertions.
    
- **Center of Gravity:** Border marches facing **Solari**; safehouse arteries toward **Zephyr** and **Calandria**.
    
- **Identity:** Anti-tyrant, pro-commons. Oaths to the **Free Charter**, not to lords.
    

---

# Political Order

| Layer | Body | What it does | How it’s checked |
|------|------|---------------|------------------|
| Confederation | **Free Conclave** (delegates from each commune + exile leagues) | Treaties, defense, mint, roads, intelligence sharing | Annual recall vote; emergency “Recall-by-Majority” at any time |
| Executive (temporary) | **Marshal of the Free** (elected 1 year, non-consecutive) | Coordinates militias, logistics, foreign missions | Can be vetoed by a 2/3 Conclave vote |
| Judiciary | **Charter Courts** (itinerant) | Adjudicate disputes, confiscations, war crimes | Citizen juries; appeal to **Grand Assembly** |
| Local | **Communal Councils** | Land commons, rations, policing, guild permits | Town-meeting override; audit brigades |


- **Charter Pillars:** 1) no hereditary titles, 2) rights of assembly & movement, 3) land for service, 4) transparency of accounts, 5) sanctuary for defectors.

# Economy (how they keep going)

### Sectors & Strengths

| Sector | Output | Edge |
|-------|--------|------|
| **Agri-Commons** | Barley, beans, hardy root veg; communal granaries | Land reform; crop rotation taught by exile agronomists |
| **Timber & Resin** | Pine, pitch, turpentine, charcoal (Hyllo & ridge forests) | Forest co-ops; sustainable quotas |
| **Metal & Repairs** | Toolmaking, refurb arms/armor, wagon/boat repair | Scavenged Solari kit + guild tinkers |
| **Smuggling & Couriering** | People, intel, high-value goods through greenways | Freeway stations; coded pilotage with Drakonia |
| **Crafts & Print** | Pamphlets, broadsheets, bindings, simple optics | Mobile presses; propaganda war experts |
| **Mercy & Medicinals** | Field surgery, herbals, poultices | Zephyrite lore + communal clinics |


### Money, Tax & Trade

- **Coin:** **Falcons** (silver), **Links** (copper). Conclave also issues **Freedom Notes** (short-term paper) backed by timber and granary reserves.
    
- **Levy:** **Commons tithe** (grain/wood days), **road-days**, light **market dues**. War contributions are time-limited and published.
    
- **Trade Partners:**
    
    - **Drakonia** (pearls, resin, pilotage) ⇄ timber, foodstuffs, repairs
        
    - **Calandria** (finance, glass, books) ⇄ paper, dyes, safe convoy routes
        
    - **Verosia** (tools, scholarship, arcanic basics) ⇄ intelligence, volunteers

# Society

| Group | Status/Rights | Obligations |
|------|----------------|-------------|
| **Free Citizens** | Vote, hold land-use shares, due process | Work-days, muster duty, jury service |
| **Exiles/Defectors** | Fast-track to citizenship after service | Oath to Charter; probation tasks |
| **Guild Leagues** | Recognized by charter; no monopolies | Publish accounts; price caps in crisis |
| **Wardens & Rangers** | Cross-commune scouts/police | Keep greenways safe; answer to Courts |
| **Sanctuary Orders** | Healers, quartermasters, clergy | Neutrality in politics; open records |


- **Faith:** Plural; many revere **freedom, oath, and memory** cults. The falcon and **broken chain** are civic symbols, not dogma.
    

---

# Security & Doctrine

- **Forces:** Citizen **militias** (archers, pike, skirmishers), **ranger companies** (counter-scouts), sapper circles, few arcanists.
    
- **Tactics:** Avoid set-piece battles. **Ambush, interdiction, sabotage, truth war** (pamphlets, songs, witness tribunals).
    
- **Infrastructure:** Greenway relay posts, beacon knolls, hidden river steps; “shadow mills” to feed on the move.

# Politics (factions inside the Free State)

| Faction | Aim | Friction |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **Radicals (“Chainbreakers”)** | Export revolt into Solari now | Risk of overreach; strain logistics |
| **Pragmatists (“Steadiers”)** | Consolidate, build roads & granaries first | Accused of softness toward nobles |
| **Reconciliators** | Bargain with border nobles; amnesties | Suspect to Radicals; useful for intel |
| **Sanctuary Bloc** | Focus on refugees, clinics, education | Resist militarization of everything |


- **Rebel Leader Marcus** sits between **Chainbreakers** and **Steadiers**; popular with militias, wary of permanent power.
