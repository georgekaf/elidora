### **Naming Style and Roleplaying Tips**

- **First Names**: Eridian first names are formal, elegant, and sometimes carry historical or literary resonance (e.g., "Alaric," "Genevieve"). These names suggest the refinement and sophistication of Eridia’s noble heritage.
- **Surnames**: Surnames often reflect the Eridian emphasis on lineage, land, and power. Names like "Ravencourt" and "Velmont" carry the gravitas of noble titles or ancestral homes.
- **Cultural Notes**: In Eridia, names are significant and often linked to family reputation and status. Many Eridians take pride in their surnames, and noble families may even adopt epithets or honorifics to emphasize their status.
- **Roleplaying Flair**: Eridians are likely formal, dignified, and proud of their heritage. Roleplaying an Eridian character might involve using eloquent speech, carefully chosen words, and an air of nobility. Even common folk in Eridia may carry themselves with an understated elegance, reflecting the cultural values of their homeland.
