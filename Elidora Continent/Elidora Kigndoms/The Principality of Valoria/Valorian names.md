### **Naming Style and Roleplaying Tips**

- **First Names**: Valorian first names often have a flowing and melodic quality, reflective of the Principality’s love for music, art, and storytelling. Names like "Seraphina," "Luciano," and "Adrian" are designed to sound beautiful when spoken.
- **Surnames**: Surnames are rich and elegant, often associated with noble families, skilled artisans, or legendary figures in Valorian history. Many surnames reflect the region's love of natural beauty, such as "Fiorenzo" (flowers) or "Montavelli" (mountains).
- **Cultural Notes**: Names in Valoria often carry deep meaning and familial pride. Many Valorian families place emphasis on honorifics and lineage, with individuals striving to live up to the legacy of their names.
- **Roleplaying Flair**: Valorians are expressive and dramatic, often speaking with grand gestures and heartfelt passion. A Valorian character may carry themselves with grace, emphasize eloquent speech, and take pride in their appearance and mannerisms. Their love for life and art often shines through in their words and actions.
