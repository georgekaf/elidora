Here’s a table-ready snapshot of the **Principality of Valoria** with strong **late-medieval → early-Renaissance Italian** vibes.

# Government & Power

- **Type:** Central **Principality** over semi-autonomous **city-states** (Signorie).
    
- **Sovereign:** **Princeps of Valoria** (hereditary), styles _Lord Protector of the Rose_.
    
- **Civic Balance:**
    
    - **Great Council** (nobles, bankers, guild priors) – taxes, treaties, mint.
        
    - **Podestà** (rotating magistrates) – law in each city; imported to curb local feuds.
        
    - **Condotta Office** – licenses **condottieri** (mercenary captains), sets pay and rules of war.
        
- **Courts & Law:** **Romanized civil codes** + city statutes; appeals to the **High Court of the Rapier** in the capital.
    

**Conspiracy nodes:**

- **Lady Fiona** – salon-patron and banker liaison; steers art patronage & opinion sheets.
    
- **Lord Granger** – condottiere magnate; controls toll roads and a “temporary” garrison near Solari.

# Economy (Renaissance engines)

|Sector|Output|Why It Thrives|
|---|---|---|
|**Banking & Exchange**|Bills of exchange, maritime insurance, prince’s loans|Deep capital pools, double-entry ledgers, Orlais & Calandria links|
|**Textiles & Dye**|Fine wool & silk, brocades, velvet|Guild mastery, imported silk thread, local madder & woad|
|**Metal & Fine Arms**|Rapier forges, plate fittings, parade armor|Court demand + export prestige|
|**Wine, Olive, Citrus**|Vintages, oils, candied peels|Terraced hills, coastal microclimates|
|**Stone & Craft**|Marble, mosaics, luthiery, glassware|Artisan academies funded by nobles & guilds|
|**Mercenary Services**|City guards, border contracts, escorts|Professional **condottieri** under state condotta rules|
|**Publishing & Theater**|Chapbooks, court comedies, political pamphlets|Literate merchant class; patron salons|

**Money & Tax:**

- Coinage: **florin** (gold), **grossi** (silver), **quattrini** (copper).
    
- Revenues: **octroi** (city gates), **tolls** on mountain passes, **export duties** on luxury cloth, **bank licenses**, **condotta fees**.
    
- Credit: Bank houses issue **letters of exchange**; state floats **Rose Bonds** for wars & public works.
    

**Trade Web (quick)**

- Buys pearls/resins/pilotage from **Drakonia**, timber & arcanic devices via **Verosia**, spices/ceramics through **Zephyr**, glass & finance with **Orlais**, and moves grain by deal-year from **Solari** (awkward but lucrative).

# Society & Culture

- **Strata:** Old nobility → _casate nuove_ (new money houses) → guild masters → artisans → day laborers.
    
- **Guilds:** Cloth, Silk, Armourers, Masons, Goldsmiths, Printers—each elect **priors**; guild courts arbitrate contracts fast.
    
- **Patronage:** Palazzi fresco cycles, public fountains, civic altarpieces; artists compete for **state commissions** (bridges, hospitals, chapels).
    
- **Università & Accademie:** Law, medicine, natural philosophy, mathematics; humanist circles copy ancient texts and write biting satires.
    
- **Religion:** High ritual; cathedrals run poorhouses and **Monti di Pietà** (charitable pawn banks) to fight usury rates.
    

---

# Security & Doctrine

- **Condottieri Companies:** Professional pike, crossbow, and light cavalry; strict contracts (pay, quartering, ransom caps).
    
- **Civic Militias:** Pike & shot emerging in big cities; watch rotations tied to guild membership.
    
- **Border Strategy:** Pay to delay, bribe to divide, fight to win prestige—**victory with minimal ruin**.

# Current Pressures (hooks)

1. **Debt & Display:** The Prince needs a triumphal bridge and a new fort; banks demand collateral on salt taxes—deal or default?
    
2. **Cloth Riots:** Silk guild vs. wool guild over tariffs; pamphlets call it a “war of sleeves.”
    
3. **Condotta Crisis:** Lord Granger’s men “accidentally” occupy a toll town; contract language is slippery—law duel or midnight heist of the seal?
    
4. **Salon Wars:** Lady Fiona bankrolls a play slandering Verosia; printers beg protection after a mob.
    
5. **Grain Gambit:** Solari grain ships delayed; Eldoria offers cheaper but riskier caravans through hill passes.

# Administrative Bits (quick use)

- **Podestà Terms:** 1 year, foreign by design, cannot marry or purchase property while seated.
    
- **Sumptuary Laws:** Limit colors & jewels by rank—easy plot hook when broken.
    
- **Public Works:** Aqueducts, loggias, hospitals funded by mixed **guild–noble consortia** (great for “where did the money go?” mysteries).
    

---

# Diplomatic Posture

Verosia – cordial patrons of learning; discreet intel sharing  
Solari  – profitable rival; treated with velvet gloves and hidden daggers  
Drakonia – hires escorts, sells pearls/resin; respect pilots  
Eldoria – buys pamphlets and sells ideals; Valoria hosts “neutral” talks  
Orlais   – finance + fashion loop; friendly competition on the seas  
Zephyr   – caravan lifelines; star-reader treaties for safe desert routes
