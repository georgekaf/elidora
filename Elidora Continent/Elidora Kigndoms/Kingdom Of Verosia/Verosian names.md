### **Naming Style and Roleplaying Tips**

- **First Names**: Verosian names carry a noble or mystical feel, with strong consonants and flowing syllables. Male names often emphasize strength or leadership, while female names highlight grace or wisdom.
- **Surnames**: Frequently tied to nature, celestial themes, or the ideals of knighthood and magic (e.g., "Silverbrook" for purity, "Stormvale" for strength, or "Duskbane" for valor).
- Verosians pride themselves on their heritage. They may introduce themselves with their full name and title, reflecting their devotion to honor, duty, or their magical legacy.
