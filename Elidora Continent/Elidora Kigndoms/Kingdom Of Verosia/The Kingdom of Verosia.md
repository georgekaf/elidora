The Kingdom of Verosia
The kingdom where the campaign starts, and where the players are from.

Conspirators: None

Organizations: 
[[The Council of the Crown Of Verosia]]

**Insignia:** A golden griffon rampant on a deep blue shield, with a crown of laurel leaves encircling its head. The griffon's talons clutch a radiant sun, symbolizing strength, wisdom, and leadership.

- **Type:** A powerful and influential kingdom in the continent of **Elidora**.
    
- **Ruler:** **King Alistair of House Velanor (and House Ravenshield)**, a wise but cautious monarch. He rules with the advice of the **Council of the Crown**, made up of powerful leaders and specialists.
    
- **Government:** A **monarchy with a ruling council**, balancing royal authority with input from mages, clerics, generals, druids, artificers, and diplomats.
    
- **Military & Power:** Known for its **knightly orders, arcane advisors, and disciplined armies**, but Verosia is less militaristic than its rival, the **Empire of Solari**. Its strength lies in **alliances, intelligence, and magical research**.
    
- **Magic:** Historically, Verosia pursued the study of **Nexus Souls** (mystical fragments tied to divine or cosmic power). This research nearly caused disaster, leading to tension with Solari, who intervened and forced them to stop. Since then, Verosia shifted to a **more peaceful path of governance and diplomacy**, but remnants of this research still exist.
    
- **Current Themes:**
    
    - Beset by **internal dissent and rebel factions**.
        
    - Facing **external pressure from Solari**, which seeks expansion and influence.
        
    - Navigating **shifting alliances** with neighboring nations like Calandria, Drakonia, and Valoria.
        
    - The Council itself may be **compromised by traitors** (notably **Archmage Arcturus**, secretly working for Solari).
