# The Sultanate of Zephyr — State Snapshot

## Government & Power

- **Form:** Central **sultanate** with vassal **oasis emirates** and caravan city-states.
    
- **Sovereign:** **Sultan of the Four Winds** (hereditary); styles _Keeper of the Athum Spires_.
    
- **Councils:**
    
    - **Divan al-Sahar** — court council (vizier, treasurer, high judge, navarch of caravan fleets).
        
    - **Majlis of Oases** — emirs & caravan-princes (water rights, tolls, route security).
        
- **Law:** **Sand Codes** (water/wayleave/blood-price/sanctuary) + **Athum Edicts** (arcane conduct near the Spires). **Judges’ caravans** ride circuit between oases.
    
- **Conspirator:** **[[Spymaster Malik]]** — coffeehouses, courier falcons, rumor markets; maintains cross-border informant webs.
    

## Economy (desert engines)

|Sector|Output|Why It Thrives|
|---|---|---|
|**Caravan Trade**|Spices, dyes, resins, silks|Control of choke-point passes & oasis chains|
|**Oasis Agriculture**|Dates, olives, millet, indigo|Qanat/cistern engineering; water rights|
|**Beastcraft & Tack**|Racing camels, barding, saddles|Selective breeding; guild standards|
|**Glass & Inlays**|Sand-glass, mosaic tiles, astrolabe parts|High-purity sands; artisan lodges|
|**Perfumes & Alchemy**|Attars, inks, salts|Apothecary guilds; Athum Spires lore|
|**Escort & Guide Services**|Scouts, outriders, star-readers|Celestial navigation traditions|
|**Salt & Copper**|Bricks, bars, alloys|Desert pans & smelter caravans|
|**Smuggling & Shadow Markets**|Black-salt, relic scrap, illicit attars, **counterfeit memory-ink**, untaxed pearls|Hidden wadis & night caravans; bribed tollkeepers; salt-pan skiff landings; **Malik’s** courier web|

**Money & tolls:** **Dinar/dirham/fulus**; wayleave tolls, oasis rents, customs at desert gates, escort licenses; **Wind Bonds** fund wells & waystations.  
**Pressure points:** Drought cycles; storm-shifted routes; bandit confederacies; rising coastal fees; Malik’s black-budget skims.

## Society & Culture

- **Strata:** Sultan’s house → oasis emirs → caravan-princes & guild sheikhs → freed folk & client tribes → travelers/metics.
    
- **Tribes & Lodges:** Lineage tribes hold water charters; urban lodges (Glass, Perfumer, Guides, Scribes) elect **naqibs** (ward leaders).
    
- **Faith & rites:** Moon-watch vigils; **Vows of Way** (traveler sanctuary); oaths sealed with **perfumed memory-ink**; festivals of **First Storm** and **Night of Lanterns**.
    
- **Dress/marks:** House-color veils/keffiyeh; bronze **crescent-and-scimitar** pins; star-reader cords knotted by constellation.
    
- **Customs:** Sunset coffeehouse debates; recited law verses; hospitality tokens (olive twig + salt grain) guarantee safe passage.
    

## Security & Doctrine

- **Forces:** **Ghazi** cavalry, **Janib** light infantry, **Sirocco** camel-scouts; mamluk garrisons on key oases.
    
- **Strategy:** **Water first** (secure wells), **Wind second** (mobility/misdirection), **Steel last** (decisive strike then withdraw).
    
- **Athum Spires:** Restricted arcane zone; wardens license researchers; relic-scrap smuggling is a priority target.
    

## Politics (living factions)

|Bloc|Aim|Tools & Tells|
|---|---|---|
|**Court of the Four Winds**|Centralize tolls & bonds|Royal inspectors; Spire edicts|
|**Oasis League**|Defend local water rights|Qanat crews; judge caravans|
|**Caravan-Princes’ Syndicate**|Cut tolls; expand private escorts|Racing festivals; coin in coffeehouses|
|**Scribes & Star-Readers**|Codify routes; preserve charts|Astrolabes; sky archives|
|**Malik’s Net**|Covert leverage across borders|Couriers; rumor markets|

## Diplomatic Posture

**Calandria** (dyes/resins; star-reader treaties) • **Orlais** (finance for Wind Bonds; convoy fees) • **Drakonia** (escort rivalry) • **Eldoria** (relief caravans) • **Solari** (watchful, limited trade)

## Hooks

1. **Wayleave War** — caravan-princes slow-roll routes over toll hikes.
    
2. **Lantern Night Theft** — a Spire lens vanishes; Malik wants it “found” on a rival.
    
3. **False Stars** — tampered **Seren tables** misroute a convoy—sabotage or error?
    
4. **Drowned Oasis** — storm-revealed cistern rewrites water rights.
    
5. **Black-Salt Sting** — trace counterfeit memory-ink from a salt-pan skiff to Malik’s couriers.
    

## Insignia

**Field:** desert orange. **Charge:** a **golden scimitar** crossed with a **crescent moon**, backed by a **swirling sandstorm**—beauty and ferocity of the desert route.
