Here’s a tight, table-ready snapshot of **the Empire of Solari**—how its economy works, and where its society & politics stand right now.

# Economy of Solari (how the machine runs)

## Core model

- **Imperial command economy over a capitalist spine.** Private guilds and merchant houses exist, but the **Sun Throne** can requisition production, fix prices on grain/iron/salt, and redirect trade during “emergency dawns” (which happen… often).
    
- **War-first budgeting.** Budgets are built around **legion readiness** and frontier works; civilian projects piggyback on military logistics.
    

### Strategic Sectors

| Sector                     | Who Controls It                         | What It Produces / Does                                  | Frictions |
|---------------------------|-----------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------|-----------|
| Grain & Staple Works      | Prefects + Grain Guild (state-capped)   | Wheat, barley, rice; imperial granaries & ration system   | Price caps strain farmers; smuggling to border towns |
| Iron & Foundries          | War Ministry + Steel Syndicate          | Legion arms, armor, siege gear                            | Ore imports vulnerable to blockade; corruption in quotas |
| Arcanum (State Magic)     | **Imperial Arcanum** (Arcturus’s network allies) | Battle-mage cadres, wards, scrying, propaganda illusions  | Talent drain from private circles; secrecy breeds graft |
| Roads, Fords, Forts       | Corps of Engineers                      | Causeways, bridges, frontier forts                        | High upkeep; labor levies unpopular |
| Textiles & Leather        | Urban guilds (state quotas)             | Uniforms, tents, harness                                 | Rural weavers crushed by quotas in bad years |
| Maritime & River Trade    | Admiralty + Licensed factors            | Convoys, coastal patrols, tariffs                         | Privateers blur line with pirates |
| Arcanite Supply (import)  | Crown monopoly (licensing few cartels)  | Enchantments, foci, arcanic engines                       | Reliant on foreign sources (Verosia/Eridia/Zephyr) |

### Money, tax, and credit

- **Currency:** **Solaris** (gold), **Lumen** (silver), **Sparks** (copper). Debasement denied by the court… but watch the border exchange rates.
    
- **Tax stack:** land tithe (in grain or coin), guild production levies, road/port tariffs, **war surtax** during campaigns.
    
- **Credit:** Imperial **Sun Bonds** underwritten by temple & great houses; contractors get paid in bonds + privileges (warehouse rights, salvage shares).
    

### Trade posture

- **Exports:** steel, finished arms, uniforms, propaganda prints, administrative scribes.
    
- **Imports:** arcanite, rare herbs/spices, timber (from Arathor/Drakonia), luxury goods (Orlais/Calandria).
    
- **Choke points:** When **Drakonia** tightens pilotage or **Arathor** raises road dues, Solari bleeds coin.

# Social State (who lives how)

## Class pyramid

| Stratum                   | What they get                                         | What’s expected of them                             |
| ------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Sun Throne & Inner Court  | Palaces, war budgets, secret networks                 | Victory, stability, omens in their favor            |
| Great Houses              | Provincial estates, monopolies, commands              | Supply quotas, raise cohorts, provide “loyal heirs” |
| Guild Masters/Factors     | Urban wealth, licenses, court access                  | Meet quotas, fund public rites, inform on rivals    |
| Legion & Arcanum          | Pay, land grants, prestige, fast justice              | Obedience, xenial propaganda, frontier tours        |
| Free Citizens             | Limited rights, ration access, guild entry            | Taxes, levies, attendance at Sun rites              |
| “Dawnsmen” (Labor levies) | Food stipends, shelter, chance of citizenship         | Road/fort works, canal dredging, siege trains       |
| Subject Peoples           | Tolerated customs, heavy tariffs, conscription quotas | Tribute, language learning, loyalty oaths           |


## Religion & culture

- **State cult:** the **Solar Phoenix** (rebirth through conquest). Public rites tie harvests to victories; dissent is framed as “eclipse-mongering.”
    
- **Civic life:** parades, Sun Games, and veterans’ colonies along new roads. Art is heroic, geometric, and **sunburst**-heavy.

# Political State (who’s actually in charge)

## Formal structure

- **Autocratic Emperor** on the **Sun Throne**.
    
- **Three great ministries:** War, Treasury, and the **Imperial Arcanum** (the quiet one).
    
- **Censors & Prefects:** provincial administrators; rotate often to break local power.
    

## Shadow structure (the conspiracy, 5 years old)

- **Archmage Arcturus**: conduit between the Arcanum and foreign plots; wants Nexus lore as strategic parity vs. Verosia.
    
- **Lord Alaric**: logistics magnate; owns half the siege trains via shell guilds; profits from “temporary” war surcharges.
    
- **Lady Sabrina**: Prefect of Letters; runs the propaganda press and censors, crafts the “Verosia = barbarian” narrative.
    

## Security state

- **Black Sun Office** (secret police): informants in guilds/temples; disappearances rare but surgical.
    
- **Border doctrine:** seize fords, burn granaries only if retreating, keep roads open at all costs.
    
- **Political justice:** trials in public, sentences in private. Exile to frontier colonies is common.
