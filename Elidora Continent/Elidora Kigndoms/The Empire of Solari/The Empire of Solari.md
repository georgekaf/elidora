The Empire of Solari
(The neighboring kingdom that the wizard has made a deal with)

The Empire of Solari is a proud and powerful nation ruled by an autocratic emperor. It boasts a vast military and has conquered many neighboring lands over the years, expanding its territory and influence. 

However, despite their military might, the Solarians feel threatened by the growing power and influence of the Kingdom of Verosia. They envy Verosia's powerful allies and fear their growing strength. 

The Solarians' hatred for Verosia has been fueled by propaganda and a longstanding belief that the Verosians are barbarians who do not deserve their lands. 
This has led to numerous conflicts and skirmishes along the border, with both sides vying for control of valuable resources and strategic positions. However, despite the Solarians' efforts to crush Verosia, the kingdom has managed to survive and even thrive, making powerful allies and garnering support from other nations.
