- **First names** are often elegant and harmonious, with vowels and softer consonants.
- **Surnames** frequently reflect noble or artisan heritage, often including "De" or "D'" as a prefix to indicate origin or family lineage.
- When roleplaying, Calandrians often speak with a refined and poetic tone, showing pride in their culture's sophistication and democratic ideals.
