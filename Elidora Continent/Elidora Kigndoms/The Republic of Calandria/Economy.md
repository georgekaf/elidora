# The Republic of Calandria

## Government & Power

- **Type:** Maritime **republic** of semi-autonomous **city-states** (Isles & Harbors).
    
- **Sovereign bodies:**
    
    - **Senate of the Isles** (patrician houses + guild seats) — treaties, coinage, lighthouse network, pilotage law.
        
    - **Citizen Assemblies** (by ward) — civic works, watch, local tariffs.
        
- **Magistracies:** **Navarch** (fleet), **Praetors** (courts), **Demarchs** (mayors), **Censors-Auditors** (finance), **Tribunes** (citizen veto).
    
- **Law:** **Twelve Lustrums** civil code (revised every five years); city statutes; **High Court of Seren** for appeals (memory-ink ledgers admissible).
    
- **Hidden hand:** A wizard has steered policy for **2 years** via **[[Merchant Guildmaster Renard]]** (port fees, shipyard contracts, broadsheet funding).
    

**Conspiracy nodes (political blocs):**

- **Concordat of Old Houses** — patrician monopolies (glass, salt, lending).
    
- **Assembly of Isles** — merchant reformers; convoy treaties, cheaper dues.
    
- **Philosopher’s League** — due process & civic education; universities & pamphlets.
    
- **Mariners’ Compact** — pilots/shipwrights/chandlers; hard line vs Drakonia in contested channels.

## Economy (maritime engines)

|Sector|Output|Why It Thrives|
|---|---|---|
|**Shipyards & Convoys**|Blue-water hulls, lighthouse maintenance|Archipelago winds; modular drydocks; Orlais insurance|
|**Glass & Optics**|Lenses, mirrors, star-table gear|Lighthouse optics + university labs|
|**Olive–Wine–Salt**|Oils, vintages, fine salt|Terraced coasts; salt pans; export routes|
|**Printing & Cartography**|Charts, broadsheets, law books|Literate ports; Senate commissions|
|**Law & Notaries**|Contracts, arbitration, maritime claims|Trusted courts; memory-inks as proof|
|**Pilotage & Harborage**|Dues, pilot services, channel beacons|Contesting Drakonia’s wider claims|
|**Education & Instruments**|Astrolabes, **Seren Tables**|University patronage; star-readers|

**Money & tax:** Coinage **stella** (gold), **luna** (silver), **obol** (copper). Revenues from **lighthouse dues**, harbor tariffs, **pilotage fees**, bank licenses, Senate-chartered monopolies. City banks issue letters of exchange; state floats **Beacon Bonds** for rebuilds.

**Trade web (quick):** Orlais finance/escorts; dyes & resins via **Zephyr**; charts & glass exported broadly; contested pilot lanes with **Drakonia**; grain via **Eldoria** caravans in lean years.

**Pressure points:** Post-storm lighthouse rebuild costs; looming **Tide Court** precedent on pilotage; refugee inflows straining guild quotas; Renard’s price-fixing cartels.

## Society & Culture

- **Strata:** Patrician houses → merchant-guilds → artisans → mariners & dock labor. Paths to citizenship via **naval service**, civic works, or guild mastery.
    
- **Guilds:** Pilots, Shipwrights, Printers, Glassmakers, Notaries—each elect **priors**; guild courts settle claims quickly.
    
- **Universities & Lyceums:** Law, astronomy, engineering; **rhetoric contests** are civic sport.
    
- **Rites (three moons):** **Seren** (archives & exams), **Vaelun** (mariner vows, conflict ethics), **Lunareth** (oaths, genealogies, memory-ink seals).
    
- **Festivals:** **First Light**, **Seren Ascendant**, **Lunareth Vigil**.
    
- **Order & crime:** Port-ward courts accept memory-ink ledgers; the **Drowned Ledger** syndicate runs black-salt and pilot bribes.
    
- **Fault lines:** Patrician monopolies vs reformers; under-represented outer isles; dockyard precarity; pamphlet wars funded by outside coin (likely Renard’s channel).
    

---

## Security & Doctrine

- **Fleet:** Patrol squadrons under the **Navarch**; convoy doctrine—**avoid, fix, finish**.
    
- **Civic Watch:** Ward militias tied to guild rosters; harbor marshals.
    
- **Border Strategy:** **Law first** (courts/contracts), **coin second** (buy time), **steel last** (decisive, limited).
    

---

## Current Pressures (hooks)

1. **Beacon Debt** — Insurers (Orlais) demand stricter convoy protocols or premiums spike.
    
2. **Pilotage Trial** — **Tide Court** ruling will set who pilots the straits; lose and revenue sinks.
    
3. **Pamphlet War** — Anonymous broadsides smear the League; printers seek Tribune protection.
    
4. **Refugee Strain** — Eldorian inflows overwhelm dock wards; quotas snap—riots or reform?
    
5. **Renard’s Web** — Evidence ties Renard to manipulated dues and shipyard bids—expose or flip him.

## Administrative Bits (quick use)

- **Praetor terms:** 2 years; barred from guild office during tenure; audited post-term by Censors.
    
- **Harbor dues:** Night-entry surcharge unless convoyed; pilot-refusal fines.
    
- **Public works:** Lighthouses & bridges funded by **guild–senate consortia** (classic “where did the money go?” plots).
