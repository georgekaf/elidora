Drakonian names reflect the strength, resilience, and fierce independence of the **Confederation of Drakonia**. These names often have sharp, guttural sounds and a harsh, commanding tone, influenced by a mix of Nordic and Slavic traditions, combined with a primal, draconic flair. Drakonians have a strong connection to the elements, particularly fire and earth, which is often reflected in their names.

### **Naming Style and Roleplaying Tips**

- **First Names**: Drakonian names evoke strength, fire, and raw elemental power. Male names are often bold and sharp, while female names balance fierceness with elegance.
- **Surnames**: They often reference physical traits, elemental forces, or draconic heritage, symbolizing pride in ancestry and natural strength (e.g., "Ironclaw" for unyielding resilience or "Ashforge" for fiery craftsmanship).
- Drakonians place great importance on their clan or family ties. Introducing oneself with both name and title (e.g., "Drazhul Flameborn, of Clan Ironclaw") is common, as they take pride in their lineage and achievements.
