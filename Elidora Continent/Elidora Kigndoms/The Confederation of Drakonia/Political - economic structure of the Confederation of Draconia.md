Here’s a compact, game-ready **political–economic blueprint** for the **Confederation of Drakonia** (Sea of Medeia jungles & marshes).

# 1) Constitutional Order

- **Type:** **Confederation of broods** (clans) bound by charter; high autonomy locally, strong coordination at sea.
    
- **Supreme Body:** **Wyrmcouncil of the Seven Scales** (Sea, Marsh, Ridge, Forge, Lore, Trade, Faith).
    
    - **Powers:** tariffs & pilotage law, muster/war levies, inter-brood courts, treaty ratification.
        
    - **Rotation:** each Scale’s elder rotates every 3 years; no back-to-back terms.
        
- **Executive (ad hoc):** **Ash-Seat (Warchair)** elected only during a declared **Muster**; command over fleets/ward-bands; dissolves when crisis ends.
    
- **Judiciary:** **Tide Courts** (traveling magistrates) + **Loreclaw Archive** (records oaths, boundaries, treaties). Appeals to a **Grand Tide** during Muster.
    

# 2) Layers of Authority

| Layer                      | Who Rules                         | What They Control                        | Checks & Balances                       |
| -------------------------- | --------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Brood (village/hold)       | Matriarch/Patriarch & council     | Land/reefs, local taxes, militia         | Guest-right law; Tide Court oversight   |
| District (harbor/causeway) | Harbor Syndic (elected/appointed) | Docks, beacons, tolls, quarantine        | Wyrmcouncil tariffs & audits            |
| Confederation              | Wyrmcouncil + offices             | Pilotage, treaties, war levy, coin marks | Grand Tide, scale rotation, oath audits |

# 3) Economic Engine

- **Core Advantage:** **Waterway mastery** (pilotage monopoly, shallow-draft shipping, causeways) + **breath-tempered metallurgy**.
    
- **Key Sectors:** pilotage & charts; fisheries & salterns; reed/resin/spice harvest; boatwrights; forge-circles (light armor, rivercraft fittings); guide/guard contracts; pearls/ambergris.
    

| Revenue Stream     | How It Works                                       | Notes                                          | Checks & Balances                       |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Pilotage Fees      | Charged by draft & distance; certified pilots only | Non-confed pilots illegal; Tide Courts enforce | Guest-right law; Tide Court oversight   |
| Harbor/Beacon Dues | Light, predictable percentages on cargo            | Funds beacons, levees, quarantine              | Wyrmcouncil tariffs & audits            |
| Salvage Tenth      | 10% on lawful wreck salvage                        | Loreclaw records priority claims               | Grand Tide, scale rotation, oath audits |
| Trade Tariffs      | Narrow band on foreign high-value goods            | Keeps ports “cheap & busy”                     |                                         |
| Muster Tithe       | Temporary levy during war/crisis                   | Ends when Ash-Seat dissolves                   |                                         |


# 4) Money & Contracts

- **Coin:** foreign coin circulates; confed mints **scale-marks** (silver) + **reef tokens** (bronze/tallies).
    
- **Credit:** **Oath-script (“scale chits”)**—clan-backed notes sealed at Tide Court; default = **mark-shame** (trade ostracism).
    
- **Standardization:** **Forge-Circles** stamp alloys and breath-tempering provenance (traceable).
    

# 5) Security & Labor

- **Forces:** small elite **ward-bands** (10–30), ancestry-tuned (fire shock, lightning relay, frost anchor); **skirmish flotillas** for swamp/reef warfare.
    
- **Public Works:** every adult owes **harbor/beacon days**; buy-outs in coin/supplies allowed.
    
- **Law:** no chattel slavery; **time-bound debt service** under Tide Court audits.
    

# 6) Trade Web (examples)

| Partner | Drakonia → | → Drakonia |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Verosia | Pilotage, salt fish, breath-tempered kits, pearls | Coin, textiles, books, licensed arcanics |
| Solari  | Spices, resins, pilots (treatied) | Grain, finished steel, luxury goods (political strings) |
| Calandria | Dyes, herbs, hull kits | Glassware, fine cloth, instruments |


# 7) Crisis Mechanics (why it holds together)

- **Muster Protocol:** Wyrmcouncil 5/7 vote → Ash-Seat named → levy triggers → privateers licensed under confed flag only.
    
- **Grand Tide (Appeal):** emergency assembly of district envoys to overrule abuses (⅔ vote).
    
- **Neutrality Infrastructure:** **Hearth-Marks** (warded waystations) funded from beacon dues; sacred guest-right reduces feud cost.
    

# 8) Factions & Frictions (hooks)

- **Sea vs. Ridge:** letters-of-marque vs. stricter honor courts.
    
- **Forge-Circles vs. Trade Scale:** export of breath-tempered patterns—profit vs. security.
    
- **Foreign Pressure:** Solari protectorate offers; Verosian alliance talks; Zephyrite smugglers probing the resin trade.
    

---

**One-liner:**  
_A waterborn league of proud broods, governed by a rotating seven-scale council, monetizing pilotage and breath-tempered craft, binding commerce to honor via Tide Courts and oath-script—nimble in peace, lethal under Muster._
