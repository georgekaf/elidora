In the world of Elidora, instead of using the term "planes of existence," you could give these realms a unique and flavorful name that ties them more closely to the lore of the setting. Here are a few suggestions:

### 1. **Realms of Essence**

- These are the fundamental layers of reality, each tied to specific aspects of existence such as life, death, magic, or chaos.

### 2. **Ethereal Spheres**

- The cosmos of Elidora is divided into ethereal spheres, with each sphere representing a different aspect of creation or destruction.

### 3. **Celestial Tapestries**

- The universe is woven together like a grand tapestry, with each thread representing a different dimension or reality.

### 4. **Auric Realms**

- Realities defined by their aura, each realm resonates with a unique energy or purpose that shapes its nature.

### 5. **Worldveils**

- The layers of existence are veils that overlay one another, each hiding a different truth or reality beneath.

### 6. **Cosmic Domains**

- Divisions of the cosmos overseen by divine or primordial powers, each with its own unique rules and aspect of existence, resonating in harmony or dissonance with the others.

### 8. **Eternal Horizons**

- The realms are seen as endless horizons, each stretching into infinity with its own distinct laws and landscapes.

### 9. **Primordial Realms**

- These are ancient and fundamental layers of reality, formed during the creation of the world by the gods or primal forces.

### 10. **Mirrors of Existence**

- Realities are like mirrors, reflecting different versions or aspects of the cosmos, each with its own distortions and truths.

### 11. **The Astral Web**

- All realms are interconnected by an invisible web of energy, and each realm represents a different node or strand in this vast cosmic network.

### 12. **Planes of Resonance**

- Existence is structured around frequencies or resonances, with each "plane" vibrating at a unique energy that defines its nature.

### 13. **Dimensions of Spirit**

- Each dimension represents a different facet of spiritual or metaphysical reality, tied to the souls and energies of the universe.

### 14. **Infinite Realms**

- Known as the boundless layers of reality, they are infinite in number and vary greatly in power, influence, and connection to Elidora.

### 15. **Sanctums of the Cosmos**

- Each "sanctum" is a sacred or profane realm that holds power over specific elements of existence, like life, death, or magic.

By using one of these terms, the cosmology of Elidora can feel unique and deeply tied to the setting's lore, rather than borrowing directly from traditional D&D terminology. Let me know if you’d like help refining one of these ideas or integrating them into your campaign!
