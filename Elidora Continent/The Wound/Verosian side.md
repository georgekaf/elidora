### **The Hag Covenant** of [[Elidora Continent/The Wound/The Wound|The Wound]]

Local legend speaks of a **covenant of hags** that rules the Verosian part of this corrupted forest.

- The hags are said to **feed on the souls and memories** of those lost to The Wound.
    
- They are keepers of forbidden knowledge, claiming to “hear” the echoes of the displaced plane.
    
- Travelers whisper that they **strike bargains**, offering power in exchange for years of life, voices, or even entire memories.
    

The hags’ presence ensures that the southern woods are avoided even by Verosian soldiers. Solari spies speak of them with dread, believing they serve no nation—only the will of The Wound itself.
