
### **The Dead Trees**

The Dead Trees form a cursed forest at the southern edge of _The Wound_. Despite their name, the trees are not lifeless—they are twisted, blackened things, warped by the unstable magic that saturates the land. Their skeletal branches blot out the sky, and though the sun may shine on the outskirts, its light is soon choked by the canopy above. In the deeper reaches, it is not sunlight but the glow of lingering arcane energy that illuminates the woods.

---

### **Nature of the Forest**

- **The Outskirts**: Sparse light, gnarled trees, a heavy silence. Animals are rare, and those that remain are pale and skittish. The environment feels oppressive but survivable.
- **The Deeper Woods**: The air grows thick, smells of ozone and iron, and the ground softens with strange fungi and glowing moss. The trees seem to shift position when unobserved, turning paths back upon themselves.
- **The Heart of the Forest**: A shifting nightmare of corrupted roots and ambient magical storms. Here, the forest is unstable—time feels stretched, whispers come from nowhere, and entire groves vanish or reappear with a blink. Few who wander this deep return unchanged.

---

### **Reputation**

- **Avoided by the sane**: Villagers, hunters, and soldiers skirt the forest. Its reputation is one of slow madness and vanishing people.
- **Sanctuary of the desperate**: Outlaws, deserters, fugitives, and those who have nothing left sometimes risk the trees, believing they can escape pursuit. Some are never seen again, while others return broken or whispering of voices in the dark.

---

### **The Hag Covenant**

The deeper one goes, the more plausible it becomes to encounter the **Covenant of Hags** that legend says rules the forest’s heart. Whether they truly command the magic or are themselves prisoners of it is uncertain. Meeting them is always dangerous: some flee, some strike bargains, some are devoured. The forest itself seems to guide the lost into their claws.

---

### **Danger of the Deep**

The Dead Trees are not simply a haunted wood—they are a place where reality itself frays. Magic surges here unpredictably:

- Spells may twist or rebound.
    
- Weather can shift between clear skies and storm in moments.
    
- A single step off the path may drop travelers into entirely new terrain.
    

The forest is alive with its own will, and entry means surrendering control.
