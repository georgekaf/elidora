Kingdoms

* **The Kingdom of Verosia** \- The kingdom where the campaign starts, and where the players are from.  
  * None of the conspirators are from Verosia.  
* **The Empire of Solari** \- The neighboring kingdom that the wizard has made a deal with.   
  * The Empire of Solari is a proud and powerful nation ruled by an autocratic emperor. It boasts a vast military and has conquered many neighboring lands over the years, expanding its territory and influence. However, despite their military might, the Solarians feel threatened by the growing power and influence of the Kingdom of Verosia. They envy Verosia's powerful allies and fear their growing strength. The Solarians' hatred for Verosia has been fueled by propaganda and a longstanding belief that the Verosians are barbarians who do not deserve their lands. This has led to numerous conflicts and skirmishes along the border, with both sides vying for control of valuable resources and strategic positions. However, despite the Solarians' efforts to crush Verosia, the kingdom has managed to survive and even thrive, making powerful allies and garnering support from other nations.

  * Archmage Arcturus: The head wizard of Solari who made the deal with the neighboring kingdom to invade Verosia.  
  * Lord Alaric: One of Arcturus' most trusted aides who helps him carry out the conspiracy.  
  * Lady Sabrina: Another of Arcturus' aides who is in charge of gathering magical artifacts for the conspiracy.  
  * Date of conspiracy: Began 5 years ago.  
* **The Dominion of Arathor** \- A small but influential country that is caught in the middle of the conspiracy.  
  * Duke Gideon of The Dominion of Arathor: A corrupt noble who is plotting against the king and queen of Arathor to gain more power.  
  * Baroness Celeste of The Dominion of Arathor: One of Gideon's co-conspirators who is in charge of recruiting soldiers for the invading army.  
  * Sir Reginald of The Dominion of Arathor: Another of Gideon's co-conspirators who is in charge of espionage and sabotage against Verosia's defenses.  
  * Date of conspiracy: Began 3 years ago.  
* **The Republic of Calandria** \- A prosperous trading nation that the wizard has manipulated for his own purposes.  
  * Merchant Guildmaster Renard: The leader of the powerful merchant guild that is secretly supporting the conspiracy with funds and resources.  
  * Date of conspiracy: Began 2 years ago.  
* **The Confederation of Drakonia** \- A powerful nation of dragonborn that has been dragged into the conflict.  
  * General Kaelthas: The dragonborn general who is leading the invading army into Verosia.  
  * Date of conspiracy: Began 1 year ago.  
* The Principality of Valoria \- A wealthy and influential country that is home to many powerful nobles.  
  * Lord Granger: One of the powerful nobles who has been bribed to support the conspiracy in exchange for favors from Arcturus.  
  * Lady Fiona: Another noble who has been bribed to support the conspiracy in exchange for political power.  
  * Date of conspiracy: Began 4 years ago.  
* **The Federation of Orlais** \- A collection of smaller nations that have banded together for protection.  
* **The Sultanate of Zephyr** \- A desert kingdom that controls a vital trade route.  
  * Spymaster Malik: The spy who is gathering information for the conspiracy and reporting back to Arcturus.

* **The Grand Duchy of Eridia** \- A once-great kingdom that is now in decline, but still has valuable resources.  
  * Minister Delacroix: A corrupt official who is secretly working with the conspiracy to gain power and influence.

Conspiracy of Archmage Arcturus

Step 1 \- The empire of Solari will secretly finance and arm rebel groups in Verosia to create instability and chaos in the kingdom.

* Conspirators  
  * Archmage Arcturus: Approves the funding and provides magical support to the rebel groups.  
  * Lady Sabrina of The Empire of Solari: Provides magical artifacts and weapons to the rebel groups.  
  * Merchant Guildmaster Renard of The Republic of Calandria: Arranges the financial transactions to fund the rebel groups.  
* Clues  
  * **1.1** Solari's Emblem on Rebel Weapons (Easy Clue): The rebel groups in Verosia are using weapons and equipment that are of higher quality and technology than what is normally available to them. Some of these weapons have the emblem of Solari engraved on them.  
  * **1.1** Trail of Money from Unknown Sources (Difficult Clue): A few high-ranking officials in Verosia have been receiving large sums of money from unknown sources. Investigation reveals that the money was transferred through several shell companies, and the trail ultimately leads back to Solari.  
  * **1.3** Coded Message of Solari's Military Commanders (Hidden Clue): A coded message intercepted by Verosian spies reveals a conversation between Solari's top military commanders discussing the success of their plan to fund the rebel groups in Verosia. The message is encoded with a complex cipher that requires specialized knowledge to decrypt.

Step 2 \- Solari will stage a false-flag attack on one of their own border towns and blame it on Verosia, using the attack as a pretext for war.

* Conspirators  
  * Archmage Arcturus: Orders the staging of the false-flag attack and coordinates the cover-up.  
  * Lord Alaric of The Empire of Solari: Carries out the attack and ensures that it appears to be carried out by Verosian forces.  
* Clues  
* **2.1** War Propaganda Posters (Easy to find): Propaganda posters and pamphlets distributed throughout Solari that depict Verosians as violent aggressors and urge citizens to support the war effort. The posters feature a striking image of the "attack" on the border town, with Verosian soldiers depicted as bloodthirsty monsters.  
* **2.2** Secret Meeting with Verosian Rebels (Difficult to find): A secret meeting between high-ranking Solari officials and a group of Verosian rebels is overheard by one of the player characters. The officials offer the rebels weapons and supplies in exchange for carrying out a sabotage mission in Verosia. The rebels agree, but only if they are paid upfront and given assurances that Solari will not betray them.  
* **2.3** Coded Message about False-Flag Attack (Hidden and very hard to find): A coded message intercepted by the players that details the plan for the false-flag attack. The message is written in a cipher that is almost impossible to crack, and includes references to secret military units and covert operations. Only by piecing together multiple clues scattered throughout the campaign can the players decode the message and uncover the truth about Solari's sinister intentions.

Step 3 \- Solari will also use their spies to spread rumors and false information that Verosia is planning to invade Solari.

* Conspirators  
  * Spymaster Malik: Spreads the rumors and false information to various kingdoms and factions.  
* Clues  
  * **3.1** Easy to find clue: State newspaper articles accusing Verosia of military buildup and secret exercises \- State propaganda: Solari's state-owned newspaper publishes articles accusing Verosia of building up its military forces on the border with Solari and conducting secret military exercises.  
  * **3.2** Difficult to find clue: Conversation between Solari officials discussing report from spy in Verosia \- "Overheard conversation": The PCs hear a conversation between two Solari officials discussing a report from a spy in Verosia who claims to have seen Verosian troops conducting drills with long-range artillery and missile systems, suggesting that Verosia is preparing to launch a preemptive strike against Solari.  
  * **3.3** Hidden and very hard to find clue: Intercepted coded message from Solari intelligence agency to foreign agent \- "Coded message from Spymaster Malik": The PCs intercept a coded message from Solari's intelligence agency to one of their agents in a neutral country, instructing them to spread rumors about Verosia's aggression and military buildup to diplomats and foreign media outlets. The message also includes instructions for the agent to fabricate evidence of Verosia's plans to invade Solari.  
    * The coded message intercepted by the PCs is a carefully crafted document, written in a special cipher known only to Solari's intelligence agency. Deciphering the message requires a skilled cryptographer, but even then, some of the code words and phrases are deliberately vague and open to interpretation.  
    * The message instructs the Solari agent to approach foreign diplomats and journalists, and to subtly influence them to publish stories about Verosia's aggressive behavior and military buildup. The agent is instructed to emphasize the supposed threat that Verosia poses to Solari, and to suggest that Solari has evidence of Verosia's plans to invade.  
    * The message goes on to provide details of the fabricated evidence, which includes forged documents, maps, and correspondence supposedly intercepted from Verosian spies. The forgeries are designed to show Verosia as a ruthless and expansionist power, bent on conquering Solari and dominating the entire region.  
    * The message ends with a warning to the agent that their actions are vital to Solari's security, and that failure to carry out their mission could result in dire consequences for the agent and their family. The agent is reminded that their loyalty to Solari must come before any personal considerations.

Step 4 \- Solari will then position their military forces along the Verosian border, making it look like they are preparing for a defensive war.

* Conspirators  
  * General Kaelthas: Leads the Solari military forces and coordinates their positioning along the Verosian border.  
* Clues  
  * **4.1** Easy to find clue: Solari Military Convoys: Verosian merchants and travelers report seeing a large number of Solari military convoys moving towards the border.  
  * **4.2** Difficult to find clue: Battle Plans in Military Outpost: A Verosian spy infiltrates a Solari military outpost and discovers battle plans for an offensive against Verosia, despite Solari's public stance of defensive preparation.  
  * **4.3** Hidden and very hard to find clue: Secret Council in Capital City with Sigil on Golden Ring: Deep in Solari's capital city, a secret council of high-ranking military officials discuss the strategy for invading Verosia and carving up its territories. This council is heavily guarded and the only way to gain access is to have a special sigil engraved on a golden ring, which only the top military brass possess.

Step 5 \- At the same time, Solari will launch covert operations to sabotage Verosia's military infrastructure and weaken their defenses.

* Conspirators  
  * Archmage Arcturus: Approves and coordinates the covert operations.  
  * Sir Reginald: Leads the espionage and sabotage operations against Verosia's military infrastructure.  
* Clues  
  * **5.1** Easy to find clue "Strange Activity in the Armory": A Verosian soldier reports seeing strange individuals lurking around the castle's armory and weapon storage areas.  
  * **5.2** Difficult to find clue “Sabotage at the Smithy”: The PCs come across a burned out smithy in a small Verosian village, which seems to have been deliberately set on fire. Upon closer inspection, they find evidence that some of the equipment in the smithy was tampered with before the fire.  
  * **5.3** Hidden and very hard to find clue "Overheard: Sabotage of the Armory's Locking Mechanism": The heroes overhear a conversation between two Solari spies who are discussing their successful sabotage of Verosia's largest armory. They mention a specific mechanism that they were able to damage, which will prevent the armory's doors from locking properly in case of an attack.  
    * The mechanism is a series of interlocking gears that control the locking mechanism of the armory doors. The spies were able to sneak into the armory undetected and tamper with one of the key gears by filing down one of the teeth, causing the gears to misalign and preventing the doors from locking properly. They also added a small amount of grease to the same gear to make it more difficult to detect the sabotage.

Step 6 \- Solari will also hire mercenaries and other foreign fighters to add to their military strength, making it appear like they are merely preparing for a defensive war.

* Conspirators  
  * Baroness Celeste: Recruits mercenaries and foreign fighters to join Solari's military forces.  
* Clues  
  * **6.1** Easy to find clue: Mercenaries' Boast: The PCs come across a group of mercenaries in a Solari-controlled town, loudly boasting about their recent hire by the Solari military. They talk openly about their mission to aid Solari in their defense against Verosia.  
  * **6.2** Difficult to find clue: Encrypted Ledger: The PCs discover a ledger in a secret Solari safe house, detailing the payment transactions to various mercenary groups and foreign fighters. The ledger is heavily encrypted and written in code, making it difficult to decipher.  
  * **6.3** Hidden and very hard to find clue: Slip of the Tongue Armory Door Sabotage: The heroes stumble upon a secret meeting between Solari military leaders and a group of foreign fighters, discussing the details of their contract and strategies for the upcoming war. One of the fighters slips up and mentions a specific code phrase, indicating that they are not just there to defend Solari but to actively participate in the invasion of Verosia.
