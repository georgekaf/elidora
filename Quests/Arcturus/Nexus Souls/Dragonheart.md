The second Nexus Soul is the heart of a mighty dragon, said to contain the raw power of dragonkind. The Dragonheart is guarded by a dragonborn paladin who believes that the Dragonheart is key to unlocking his true potential as a dragon slayer. To convince the paladin to give up the Dragonheart, the players may need to prove that they are capable of using the Dragonheart to protect the realm from other threats.
