The tenth and final Nexus Soul is not a soul, but a living being known as The Last Guardian. The Last Guardian is a powerful entity that has been entrusted with the responsibility of protecting the Nexus Souls from falling into the wrong hands. The players may encounter [[The Last Guardian]] at some point in their journey, and they may need to convince them to entrust them with the Nexus Souls. The Last Guardian is a formidable opponent, but they are not without their weaknesses. The players may need to uncover their motivations and offer them a compelling reason to hand over the Nexus Souls. Alternatively, they may need to defeat The Last Guardian in combat to retrieve the Nexus Souls by force. However, this approach could have dire consequences, as it may result in the destruction of the Nexus Souls and the release of their energies into the world.
