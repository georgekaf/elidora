In the heart of Verosia, whispers of betrayal and shadowy dealings emerge, threatening the kingdom's stability. Tasked with uncovering the truth, the heroes must delve into a web of lies, unearth hidden alliances, and expose a conspiracy that seeks to unravel the realm from within. As they navigate treacherous politics, cryptic clues, and deadly adversaries, the fate of Verosia hangs in the balance—can they uncover the truth before it's too late?

Step 1 - The empire of Solari will secretly finance and arm rebel groups in Verosia to create instability and chaos in the kingdom.
Conspirators

[[Archmage Arcturus]]s: Approves the funding and provides magical support to the rebel groups.
[[Lady Sabrina]] of The Empire of Solari: Provides magical artifacts and weapons to the rebel groups.
[[Merchant Guildmaster Renard]] of The Republic of Calandria: Arranges the financial transactions to fund the rebel groups.

Clues
[[1.1 Solari's Emblem on Rebel Weapons]] (Easy Clue): The rebel groups in Verosia are using weapons and equipment that are of higher quality and technology than what is normally available to them. Some of these weapons have the emblem of Solari engraved on them.
[[1.2 Trail of Money from Unknown Sources]] (Difficult Clue): A few high-ranking officials in Verosia have been receiving large sums of money from unknown sources. Investigation reveals that the money was transferred through several shell companies, and the trail ultimately leads back to Solari.
[[1.3 Coded Message of Solari's Military Commanders]] (Hidden Clue): A coded message intercepted by Verosian spies reveals a conversation between Solari's top military commanders discussing the success of their plan to fund the rebel groups in Verosia. The message is encoded with a complex cipher that requires specialized knowledge to decrypt.

Step 2 - Solari will stage a false-flag attack on one of their own border towns and blame it on Verosia, using the attack as a pretext for war.
Conspirators
[[Archmage Arcturus]]: Orders the staging of the false-flag attack and coordinates the cover-up.
[[Lord Alaric]] of The Empire of Solari: Carries out the attack and ensures that it appears to be carried out by Verosian forces.

Clues
[[2.1 War Propaganda Posters]] (Easy to find): Propaganda posters and pamphlets distributed throughout Solari that depict Verosians as violent aggressors and urge citizens to support the war effort. The posters feature a striking image of the "attack" on the border town, with Verosian soldiers depicted as bloodthirsty monsters.
[[2.2 Secret Meeting with Verosian Rebels]] (Difficult to find): A secret meeting between high-ranking Solari officials and a group of Verosian rebels is overheard by one of the player characters. The officials offer the rebels weapons and supplies in exchange for carrying out a sabotage mission in Verosia. The rebels agree, but only if they are paid upfront and given assurances that Solari will not betray them.
[[2.3 Coded Message about False-Flag Attack]] (Hidden and very hard to find): A coded message intercepted by the players that details the plan for the false-flag attack. The message is written in a cipher that is almost impossible to crack, and includes references to secret military units and covert operations. Only by piecing together multiple clues scattered throughout the campaign can the players decode the message and uncover the truth about Solari's sinister intentions.

Step 3 - Solari will also use their spies to spread rumors and false information that Verosia is planning to invade Solari.
Conspirators
[[Spymaster Malik]]: Spreads the rumors and false information to various kingdoms and factions.
Clues
[[3.1 State propaganda]] Easy to find clue: State newspaper articles accusing Verosia of military buildup and secret exercises - State propaganda: Solari's state-owned newspaper publishes articles accusing Verosia of building up its military forces on the border with Solari and conducting secret military exercises.
[[3.2 Overheard conversation]] Difficult to find clue: Conversation between Solari officials discussing report from spy in Verosia - "Overheard conversation": The PCs hear a conversation between two Solari officials discussing a report from a spy in Verosia who claims to have seen Verosian troops conducting drills with long-range artillery and missile systems, suggesting that Verosia is preparing to launch a preemptive strike against Solari.
[[3.3 Coded message]] Hidden and very hard to find clue: Intercepted coded message from Solari intelligence agency to foreign agent - "Coded message from Spymaster Malik": The PCs intercept a coded message from Solari's intelligence agency to one of their agents in a neutral country, instructing them to spread rumors about Verosia's aggression and military buildup to diplomats and foreign media outlets. The message also includes instructions for the agent to fabricate evidence of Verosia's plans to invade Solari.
The coded message intercepted by the PCs is a carefully crafted document, written in a special cipher known only to Solari's intelligence agency. Deciphering the message requires a skilled cryptographer, but even then, some of the code words and phrases are deliberately vague and open to interpretation.
The message instructs the Solari agent to approach foreign diplomats and journalists, and to subtly influence them to publish stories about Verosia's aggressive behavior and military buildup. The agent is instructed to emphasize the supposed threat that Verosia poses to Solari, and to suggest that Solari has evidence of Verosia's plans to invade.
The message goes on to provide details of the fabricated evidence, which includes forged documents, maps, and correspondence supposedly intercepted from Verosian spies. The forgeries are designed to show Verosia as a ruthless and expansionist power, bent on conquering Solari and dominating the entire region.
The message ends with a warning to the agent that their actions are vital to Solari's security, and that failure to carry out their mission could result in dire consequences for the agent and their family. The agent is reminded that their loyalty to Solari must come before any personal considerations.

Step 4 - Solari will then position their military forces along the Verosian border, making it look like they are preparing for a defensive war.
Conspirators
[[General Kaelthas]]: Leads the [[Solarian Army]] and [[Drakonian Army]] military forces and coordinates their positioning along the Verosian border.
Clues
[[4.1 Solari Military Convoys]] (Easy to find clue): Verosian merchants and travelers report seeing a large number of Solari military convoys moving towards the border.
[[4.2 Battle Plans in Military Outpost]] Difficult to find clue: A Verosian spy infiltrates a Solari military outpost and discovers battle plans for an offensive against Verosia, despite Solari's public stance of defensive preparation.
[[4.3 Secret Council in Capital City with Sigil on Golden Ring]] Hidden and very hard to find clue: Deep in Solari's capital city, a secret council of high-ranking military officials discuss the strategy for invading Verosia and carving up its territories. This council is heavily guarded and the only way to gain access is to have a special sigil engraved on a golden ring, which only the top military brass possess.

Step 5 - At the same time, Solari will launch covert operations to sabotage Verosia's military infrastructure and weaken their defenses.
Conspirators
[[Archmage Arcturus]]: Approves and coordinates the covert operations.
[[Sir Reginald of The Dominion of Arathor]]: Leads the espionage and sabotage operations against Verosia's military infrastructure.
Clues
[[5.1 Strange Activity in the Armory]] Easy to find clue: A Verosian soldier reports seeing strange individuals lurking around the castle's armory and weapon storage areas.
[[5.2 Sabotage at the Smithy]] Difficult to find clue: The PCs come across a burned out smithy in a small Verosian village, which seems to have been deliberately set on fire. Upon closer inspection, they find evidence that some of the equipment in the smithy was tampered with before the fire.
[[5.3 Overheard - Sabotage of the Armory's Locking Mechanism]]: Hidden and very hard to find clue The heroes overhear a conversation between two Solari spies who are discussing their successful sabotage of Verosia's largest armory. They mention a specific mechanism that they were able to damage, which will prevent the armory's doors from locking properly in case of an attack.
The mechanism is a series of interlocking gears that control the locking mechanism of the armory doors. The spies were able to sneak into the armory undetected and tamper with one of the key gears by filing down one of the teeth, causing the gears to misalign and preventing the doors from locking properly. They also added a small amount of grease to the same gear to make it more difficult to detect the sabotage.

Step 6 - Solari will also hire mercenaries and other foreign fighters to add to their military strength, making it appear like they are merely preparing for a defensive war.
Conspirators
[[Baroness Celeste of The Dominion of Arathor]]: Recruits mercenaries and foreign fighters to join Solari's military forces.
Clues
[[6.1 Mercenaries' Boast]] Easy to find clue: The PCs come across a group of mercenaries in a Solari-controlled town, loudly boasting about their recent hire by the Solari military. They talk openly about their mission to aid Solari in their defense against Verosia.
[[6.2 Encrypted Ledger]] Difficult to find clue: The PCs discover a ledger in a secret Solari safe house, detailing the payment transactions to various mercenary groups and foreign fighters. The ledger is heavily encrypted and written in code, making it difficult to decipher.
[[6.3 Slip of the Tongue]] Hidden and very hard to find clue: Armory Door Sabotage: The heroes stumble upon a secret meeting between Solari military leaders and a group of foreign fighters, discussing the details of their contract and strategies for the upcoming war. One of the fighters slips up and mentions a specific code phrase, indicating that they are not just there to defend Solari but to actively participate in the invasion of Verosia.

Step 7 - When the time is right, Solari will launch a surprise attack on Verosia, catching them off guard and quickly conquering their territory.
Conspirators
[[Archmage Arcturus]]: Leads the Solari military forces and coordinates the surprise attack on Verosia.
[[Lord Alaric]]: Leads a unit of troops and infiltrates key Verosian defense positions.
Clues
[[7.1 Military Buildup]] Easy to find clue: Increased Solari military activity: There is a noticeable increase in Solari military activity near the Verosian border, including more patrols and troop movements.
[[7.2 Border Sightings]] Difficult to find clue: Verosian scout report: A Verosian scout reports seeing a large and well-armed Solari army gathering just beyond the border, but they were unable to get a clear count of the enemy forces or discern their specific intentions.
[[7.3 Operation Sunfire Intel]]  Hidden and very hard to find clue: Overheard Solari officers discussing the invasion: A group of Verosian refugees, who managed to escape a recent Solari conquest, reveal to the heroes that they overheard Solari officers discussing their plans for a surprise attack on Verosia. The officers referred to the attack as "Operation Sunfire" and mentioned a specific date for the invasion. However, the date was spoken in code and would require knowledge of Solari military tactics to decipher.

In the [[Archmage Arcturus]] kingdom, the most prominent conspirator is [[Lord Vayne]], the wizard's trusted advisor and closest confidante. Vayne is a powerful mage in his own right, and has been working with the wizard for many years to find a way to harness the power of the Nexus. He is also the one who made the deal with the neighboring kingdom, promising them access to the Nexus in exchange for their support.
Other key conspirators within the wizard's kingdom include:
[[Baroness Lyra]], a wealthy and influential noblewoman who has been promised even greater wealth and power in exchange for her support.
[[Lord Darion]], the commander of the wizard's army, who has been bribed with promises of land and titles in the neighboring kingdom.
[[Lady Isadora - Mistress of the Arcane Arts]], the head of the wizard's spy network, who has been feeding information to the neighboring kingdom in exchange for gold.
In the neighboring kingdom, the key conspirators include:
[[King Roderick]], the ruler of the neighboring kingdom, who sees the opportunity to gain power and wealth by invading the wizard's kingdom.
[[Lord Alaric]], the commander of the neighboring kingdom's army, who has been promised the lands of the wizard's kingdom as a reward for his support.
[[Lady Mirella]], a powerful sorceress who has been promised access to the Nexus in exchange for her help in the invasion.
Other individuals who have been implicated in the conspiracy include:
[[Lord Hector]], a noble from a neighboring country who has been promised land and titles in the wizard's kingdom in exchange for his support.
[[Lady Sariel]], a diplomat from yet another country who has been promised access to the wizard's kingdom's rich trade routes in exchange for her help in convincing other countries to stay out of the conflict.
