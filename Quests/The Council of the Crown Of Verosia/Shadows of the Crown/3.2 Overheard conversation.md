
[[Master Artificer Thorne, Keeper of the Clockwork Secrets]], Keeper of the Clockwork Secrets - Needs the heroes to retrieve a stolen clockwork automaton and its plans, which were stolen by a rival artificer.

The quest begins with [[Master Artificer Thorne, Keeper of the Clockwork Secrets]] explaining to the heroes the importance of retrieving the stolen clockwork automaton and its plans from [[Lyssandra]], the rival artificer. Thorne explains that the automaton is a highly advanced prototype that has never been seen before and contains some of Verosia's most guarded secrets. The automaton is also highly sought after by rebels who work against Verosia and would stop at nothing to get their hands on it.

The clockwork automaton is a marvel of artifice and engineering, standing at about five feet tall. Its frame is made of a combination of brass and steel, giving it a sturdy and durable appearance. Its joints and limbs move with a smooth precision, and its fingers are delicate enough to perform intricate tasks. The automaton's head is designed to resemble that of a human, complete with glowing, arcane-powered eyes that seem to follow its surroundings. Its chest cavity is filled with a series of cogs, gears, and springs, which regulate its movements and power its abilities. It is adorned with intricate details and embellishments, such as engravings, filigree, and polished gemstones.

As the heroes and [[Thorne]] investigate [[Lyssandra]]'s whereabouts, they discover that she has been selling information to [[Solari officials]] from within Verosia. Thorne reveals that the clockwork automaton was designed to be a powerful weapon, and if it were to fall into Solari's hands, it could tip the balance of power in their favor.

Thorne provides the heroes with a map of Lyssandra's house and the abandoned warehouse where the automaton is being kept. However, Lyssandra has heavily fortified her house, and getting in undetected will be a challenge. The heroes will need to come up with a plan to either sneak in or distract Lyssandra's guards to gain access to the plans.

Once the heroes have the plans, they will need to make their way to the abandoned warehouse and retrieve the automaton. However, they soon discover that [[Solari officials]] have stationed guards around the warehouse, likely to make their move soon.

As the heroes make their escape with the automaton, they overhear a conversation between two Solari officials discussing a report from a spy in Verosia who claims to have seen Verosian troops conducting drills, suggesting that Verosia is preparing to launch a preemptive strike against Solari.

This causes the heroes to realize that Solari's plan is to stage a false-flag attack and blame it on Verosia. They must race back to Thorne and Lady Althea to warn them of Solari's plan before it's too late. With the plans and automaton in their possession, the heroes are now in a race against time to stop a war from breaking out between Verosia and Solari.
