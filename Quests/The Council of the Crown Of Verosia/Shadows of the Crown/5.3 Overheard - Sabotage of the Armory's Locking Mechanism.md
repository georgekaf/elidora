The heroes are summoned to the castle by [[Lord Master Taranis Oakstaff]], who is concerned about reports of strange behavior among the kingdom's wildlife. He suspects that there might be a supernatural cause behind it and asks the heroes to investigate.

During their investigation, the heroes come across an abandoned campsite deep in the forest. Upon further investigation, they find evidence that suggests that the campsite was recently used by two Solari spies. The evidence includes empty food containers, discarded Solari uniforms, and footprints leading away from the campsite.

The heroes follow the footprints, which lead them to a hidden cave deep in the forest. They overhear a conversation between two Solari spies who are discussing their successful sabotage of Verosia's largest armory. The heroes learn about the specific mechanism that was damaged and the method used to carry out the sabotage.

The supernatural cause would be a magical artifact the two solari spies are placing in the cave to drive away living beings in order to reduce wildlife, the forest is the favourite hunting spot of many soldiers.

To tie it together with the sabotage of the armory, it's possible that the solari spies were working in conjunction with [[Sir Reginald of The Dominion of Arathor]], who is responsible for espionage and sabotage against Verosia's defenses. Perhaps they were using the artifact as a distraction to draw attention away from their main goal, which was to sabotage the armory's locking mechanism.

The heroes will have to investigate the solari spies and the artifact to see if there is a link between them and the armory sabotage. It's possible that the solari spies were hired by [[Sir Reginald of The Dominion of Arathor]] to distract the kingdom's forces while he carried out his sabotage mission.

The Solari spies are armed and dangerous, so the heroes will need to use stealth and strategy to confront them. Once the spies are dealt with, the heroes can report back to Lord Master Taranis Oakstaff and provide him with the information they discovered about the sabotage of the armory's locking mechanism.
