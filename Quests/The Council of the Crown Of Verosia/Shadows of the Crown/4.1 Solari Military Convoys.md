High Council member [[Archsage Alder]] of the Kingdom of Verosia, Keeper of the Cosmic Secrets - Needs the heroes to travel to the far reaches of the kingdom to retrieve a lost tome of cosmic knowledge.

As the heroes set out on their quest to retrieve the lost tome of cosmic knowledge, they quickly hear rumors of Solari military convoys moving towards the Verosian border. While initially dismissed as routine military exercises, it soon becomes clear that the Solari forces are preparing for something more significant.

As they journey deeper into the kingdom, the heroes encounter various obstacles and challenges, from treacherous terrain to hostile creatures. Along the way, they also come across clues that suggest Solari's true intentions - they are preparing for an all-out invasion of Verosia.

The easy to find clue of Solari military convoys proves to be true as the heroes come across the convoys themselves. However, they also discover that the convoys are carrying not just soldiers, but also siege engines and other weapons of war.

As they continue their journey, the heroes come across more evidence of Solari's aggression, from destroyed Verosian outposts to captured soldiers. They realize that the tome of cosmic knowledge is not just a mere artifact but holds great power and knowledge that could tip the balance of power in the impending war.

The final leg of the journey takes the heroes to the edge of the kingdom, where they discover the lost tome of cosmic knowledge. However, they are not alone - a group of Solari soldiers have also discovered the tome and are attempting to take it back to their kingdom.
