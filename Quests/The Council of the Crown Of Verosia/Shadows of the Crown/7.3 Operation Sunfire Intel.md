Quest: [[King Alistair of House Velanor]] of the [[The Kingdom of Verosia]] has received word of an imminent threat to the kingdom, and he needs a group of heroes to investigate and gather information about the potential danger.

The heroes are summoned to the royal court where [[King Alistair of House Velanor]] informs them of the rumors he has been hearing about a possible attack on Verosia. He explains that he needs them to travel to neighboring kingdoms and gather any information they can about the possible threat. The King stresses the importance of discretion and warns the heroes to avoid drawing attention to themselves.

As the heroes begin their mission, they encounter a group of Verosian refugees who managed to escape a recent Solari conquest. The refugees reveal that they overheard Solari officers discussing their plans for a surprise attack on Verosia. The officers referred to the attack as "Operation Sunfire" and mentioned a specific date for the invasion. However, the date was spoken in code and would require knowledge of Solari military tactics to decipher.

The heroes must now use their skills to gather information and decipher the code to uncover the exact date and details of the impending attack. They must also be cautious not to draw attention to themselves or alert the Solari forces of their investigations.

Upon their return to the kingdom, the heroes must report their findings to King Alistair and devise a plan to defend Verosia against the attack. The fate of the kingdom rests in their hands.

The heroes will need to gather information through a variety of means, such as infiltrating Solari military outposts or camps, interrogating prisoners, and gathering intelligence from friendly spies. They will need to keep their wits about them and be careful not to get caught or draw unwanted attention.

To decipher the code for the specific date of the invasion, the heroes may need to consult with experts in Solari military tactics or break into Solari code books to decode the message. They may also need to gather other intelligence to uncover the purpose and target of the attack, as well as the size and composition of the Solari forces involved.

Once the heroes have gathered enough information, they will need to report back to King Alistair with their findings and work with him to develop a plan to defend the kingdom against the impending attack. This may involve calling up additional troops, fortifying strategic positions, and developing tactics to counter the strengths of the invading Solari forces. The heroes may also be called upon to take a leading role in the defense effort, using their skills and expertise to help repel the invasion and defend the kingdom.
