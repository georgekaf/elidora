[[Lady Althea, Mistress of Poisons and Cures]] is desperate to find the rare medicinal plants that can cure the deadly plague that is ravaging Verosia. She informs the heroes that there is a rare plant that grows deep in the forest, and it is the key ingredient for the cure. However, the plant is guarded by a tribe of secretive and territorial forest-dwelling people known as the Caelan. [[The Caelan]] are known to be hostile to outsiders and have never allowed anyone to take the plant before.

The heroes must navigate through the dense forest and find the Caelan village. There, they must convince the tribe to let them take the plant. However, the Caelan are wary of outsiders and will only trust those who can prove their worth. The heroes must complete a series of challenges and tasks, proving their bravery, intelligence, and resourcefulness.

As they journey through the forest, the heroes come across a group of Solari spies who are spreading rumors about Verosia's plan to invade Solari. The spies are disguised as traders and are offering to sell weapons and armor to the Caelan. However, the heroes overhear the conversation and realize that the spies are actually trying to convince the Caelan to attack Verosian trade routes.

The heroes must stop the spies from spreading false information and convince the Caelan that Verosia is not planning to invade Solari. They also discover that the spies have a coded message with instructions to fabricate evidence of Verosia's plans to invade Solari. The heroes must decipher the message and bring it back to [[Lady Althea, Mistress of Poisons and Cures]].

With the rare plant in hand and the coded message decoded, the heroes return to Lady Althea. She is able to use the plant to create a cure for the plague, and the people of Verosia are saved. 

The decoded message is presented to the [[The Council of the Crown Of Verosia]], who are able to disprove the false information and prevent Solari from starting a war. The heroes are hailed as heroes, and [[Lady Althea, Mistress of Poisons and Cures]] rewards them with valuable artifacts and knowledge of her expertise.

TIP: The encoded message comes from the [[Spymaster Malik]]
