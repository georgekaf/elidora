[[Lady Celestria, Voice of the Heavens]], Voice of the Heavens - Has had a vision of a coming disaster and needs help to prevent it. The heroes must investigate the vision and prevent the disaster from occurring.

"[[The Holy City of Celestia]] has been peaceful for centuries, but [[Lady Celestria, Voice of the Heavens]], Voice of the Heavens, has received a troubling vision. She sees the city being destroyed by a massive earthquake, which will cause thousands of deaths and bring great suffering to the people. [[Lady Celestria, Voice of the Heavens]] believes that the disaster can be prevented if the heroes can find and deactivate a powerful magical artifact that is causing the earthquake.

The heroes must start by investigating the source of the earthquake, which seems to be coming from deep beneath the city. They will encounter many obstacles along the way, including treacherous tunnels, deadly traps, and fierce monsters. They will also need to use their wits to solve puzzles and decipher cryptic clues left by the artifact's creator.

As they get closer to the artifact, the heroes will encounter strange individuals who seem to be profiting from the impending disaster. They learn that these individuals are receiving large sums of money from unknown sources and suspect that they might be involved in causing the earthquake. The heroes will need to gather evidence to prove their guilt and stop them from carrying out their plan.

Finally, the heroes reach the artifact, which is hidden deep within a labyrinthine dungeon. 
Outcomes:
 - Success: They must fight their way through waves of powerful guardians to reach the artifact and deactivate it before it causes the earthquake, as they make their way out of the city, the heroes encounter [[Lady Celestria, Voice of the Heavens]], who thanks them for their bravery and tells them that their actions have saved countless lives. The heroes are hailed as heroes and are granted many rewards for their heroic deeds."

- Fail: If they fail to find the artifcact or deactivcate it. they must race against time to evacuate part of the city before the earthquake strikes. If they fail, [[Lady Sabrina]] will not blame them per se but she won't consider them as reliable anymore.

As the heroes delve deeper into their investigation of the earthquake's source, they encounter several strange individuals who seem to be profiting from the disaster. Through their questioning, they uncover evidence of a conspiracy involving high-ranking officials in [[The Kingdom of Verosia]] who have been receiving large sums of money from unknown sources.

Major Plothooks: 
The evidence is difficult to find, but the heroes' persistence pays off as they come across a half-burned ledger book and notes that suggest that the money was transferred through several shell companies and ultimately leads back to [[The Empire of Solari]]. Through further investigation, they discover that the funds were used to create and transport the powerful magical artifact that is causing the earthquake.

They learn that a conspirator named [[Merchant Guildmaster Renard]] of [[The Republic of Calandria]] arranges the financial transactions to fund the rebel groups responsible for creating the artifact.

The heroes must dig deep to uncover this lead, which will be particularly difficult to find, but they are determined to gather the evidence needed to prove their guilt and stop them from carrying out their plan.
The ledger book and burned notes provide a crucial piece of evidence that the heroes need to piece together the conspiracy and stop the disaster from occurring. They use this information to confront the conspirators and ultimately deactivate the magical artifact, preventing the earthquake and saving countless lives. In the end, their heroic deeds are recognized, and they are hailed as saviors of the holy city of Celestia.

To further conceal the transfer, [[Merchant Guildmaster Renard]] might use coded language or aliases in the ledger book and notes. He could also employ other methods such as using different currencies or disguising the transactions as legitimate business expenses or donations.
