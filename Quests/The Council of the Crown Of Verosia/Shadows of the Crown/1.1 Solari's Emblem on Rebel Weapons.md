[[High Magus Zephyrion]] - Has been experimenting with ancient arcane artifacts and needs help gathering materials for his next experiment. The heroes must retrieve rare and dangerous magical components from a dangerous dungeon.

1.1 Solari's Emblem on Rebel Weapons

The heroes receive a summons to the tower of [[High Magus Zephyrion]], a renowned mage who has been experimenting with ancient arcane artifacts. Upon arriving, they are greeted by [[High Magus Zephyrion]], who explains that he requires their help in collecting rare and dangerous magical components for his next experiment. He gives them a map that leads them to a dangerous dungeon filled with traps, puzzles, and fierce monsters where the components are located.

As the heroes make their way through the dungeon, they encounter various obstacles, including puzzles, and battles with dangerous creatures. They use their combat skills and magical abilities to overcome each challenge, and eventually arrive at the inner sanctum of the dungeon. 

There, they find the rare and dangerous magical components that [[High Magus Zephyrion]] needs for his experiment, but they also stumble upon a group of rebels who have been using the components to create powerful weapons.

The rebels can be captured, interrogated and they will reveal that they have been receiving information about magical artifacts and weapons from [[Lady Sabrina]], a powerful mage from the [[The Empire of Solari]], who is providing them with support against [[The Kingdom of Verosia]]. Upon hearing this information, the heroes must decide whether to report this to the Verosian authorities or keep it to themselves.

Success: The heroes either complete their mission and deliver the components to [[High Magus Zephyrion]]. In return, he rewards them with a powerful magical artifact. 
The heroes can also report [[Lady Sabrina]]'s involvement in providing support to the rebels, leading to an investigation and potential consequences for [[The Empire of Solari]]'s actions.

Fail: The heroes fail to retrieve the magical components. No rewards from [[High Magus Zephyrion]]. They might discover the link to [[Lady Sabrina]] but they won't get any reward  if they decide to report this to the Verosian authorities or keep it to themselves.
