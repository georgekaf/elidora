[[Sir Tristan Goldenheart]]: Train a group of young knights in the ways of chivalry and honor, and lead them on a mission to defend the honor of the kingdom against a group of dishonorable knights.

The High Council Member [[Sir Tristan Goldenheart]] summons the heroes and briefs them on the situation with the dishonorable knights who were expelled from the Verosian knighthood. He tells them that these knights have been causing trouble in the towns and harassing [[Verosian scouts]], and they need to be stopped before they cause any more harm.

[[Sir Tristan Goldenheart]] assigns the heroes the mission to track down the dishonorable knights and teach them a lesson, while also training the young knights in the ways of chivalry and honor. The heroes must locate the dishonorable knights and convince them to leave Verosia, or if necessary, use force to remove them from the kingdom.

While gathering information on the dishonorable knights' whereabouts, the heroes learn about the Verosian scout report detailing the large and well-armed Solari army gathering just beyond the border. However, the scout report is difficult to find as it was kept hidden by the scout in fear of retaliation by the dishonorable knights.

To obtain the scout report, the heroes must track down the Verosian scout and convince them to hand over the report. However, the scout is being harassed by the dishonorable knights, who have taken up residence in the nearby town. The heroes must teach the dishonorable knights a lesson and put an end to their harassment, while also showing the young knights the importance of upholding the code of chivalry and honor
