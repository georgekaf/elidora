[[Sir Percival Stormwind]]: Apprehend a notorious bandit leader who has been terrorizing the kingdom's highways and bring them to justice.

The quest is assigned to the heroes by [[Sir Percival Stormwind]], who has received numerous reports of attacks by a notorious bandit leader on Verosian highways. The bandit leader, known as "[[The Black Viper]]" is rumored to be hiding out in a nearby forest, and Sir Percival believes that capturing him will put a stop to the attacks.

As the heroes make their way towards the forest, they come across a group of mercenaries in a Verosian-controlled town. The mercenaries are boasting about their recent hire by the Solari military, and they talk openly about their mission to aid Solari in their defense against Verosia. The heroes may be able to gather information about the mercenaries' plans and the extent of Solari's military preparation.

As the heroes continue their investigation, they may discover that there is a connection between the Black Viper and the mercenaries. Perhaps [[The Black Viper]] has been working as a hired gun for the Solari military, using his knowledge of Verosia's highways to disrupt their supply lines. Alternatively, the mercenaries may be working for a rival kingdom and have hired the Black Viper to cause chaos in Verosia.

Either way, the heroes must capture[[The Black Viper]] and bring him to justice, while also gathering any information they can about the mercenaries and Solari's military plans. The mission becomes even more urgent when they discover that the mercenaries are planning to launch a surprise attack on a Verosian stronghold in the near future.
