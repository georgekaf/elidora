
The Guardians of the nexus souls play a crucial role in the conspiracy orchestrated by Archmage Arcturus. 

Each Guardian is responsible for protecting a nexus soul, which is a source of great magical power. Arcturus seeks to obtain all ten nexus souls in order to gain incredible power and dominance over the kingdoms.

The Guardians are initially unaware of Arcturus' plans, but he sends agents to manipulate them into turning against their kingdoms and joining his cause. The agents offer the Guardians promises of wealth, power, and protection in exchange for their assistance.

Once the Guardians are convinced to join Arcturus, they provide him with valuable information and aid in obtaining the nexus souls. They use their positions of power within their kingdoms to manipulate events in Arcturus' favor, such as influencing key decisions and suppressing dissent.

However, some of the Guardians may become disillusioned with Arcturus and his true intentions. They may try to back out of the conspiracy or even turn against Arcturus, making them targets for his wrath.

[[Arielle Arctos]] and her parents are the descendants of this race.
