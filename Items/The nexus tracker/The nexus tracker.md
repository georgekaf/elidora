The Nexus Tracker is a set of three items that work together to locate the Nexus Souls:

[[Tome with empty pages]]
[[Crystal Compass]]
[[Vial of Luminita liquid]]

Breaking the Nexus Tracker into multiple items would involve separating these three components. The crystal compass would remain as it is, while the book with empty pages could be a separate item. The vial of liquid could also be its own separate item, with the added danger and difficulty of obtaining it. Each of these items would be necessary to locate and record the Nexus Souls, and without all three, the process would be incomplete.
